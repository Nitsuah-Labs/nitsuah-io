## Image Sources & Guidelines

### Recommended Sources

- [**Unsplash**](https://unsplash.com/) - Free high-quality photos
- [**Pexels**](https://www.pexels.com/) - Free stock photos & videos
- [**Pixabay**](https://pixabay.com/) - Free images and vectors
- [**Lorem Picsum**](https://picsum.photos/) - Placeholder images for development
- [**Generated Art**](https://www.generated.photos/) - Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion for unique images

### Image Specifications

- **Format**: PNG for screenshots/UI, JPG for photos, GIF for animations
- **Optimization**: Compress images using TinyPNG or ImageOptim
- **Naming**: Use descriptive kebab-case names (e.g., `italian-pasta-carbonara.jpg`)
- **Storage**: Place in appropriate `_assets` folder
